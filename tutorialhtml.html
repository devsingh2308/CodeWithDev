Learn HTML from Scratch

HTML (HyperText Markup Language) is the foundation of every website on the internet. Whether you're building a simple personal blog or a large-scale web application, HTML is the first step in bringing your content to life.

In this tutorial series, you will learn all the essential elements of HTML — from the basic structure of a webpage to advanced semantic elements, forms, and multimedia integration.

Understanding HTML is not just about writing tags. It’s about learning how to structure information for users and browsers in a meaningful way. This makes your content accessible, readable, and search-engine friendly.

We’ll walk you through topics such as:

  • Headings, paragraphs, and lists
  • Links and navigation
  • Images, videos, and iframes
  • Forms, inputs, and buttons
  • Tables and semantic HTML5 elements

By the end of this tutorial, you'll have a solid understanding of HTML and be ready to move on to CSS and JavaScript for building full-fledged websites.

Whether you're a complete beginner or brushing up your skills, this guide is a perfect place to start your web development journey.
